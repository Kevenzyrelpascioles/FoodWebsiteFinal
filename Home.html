Chicken Pepperoni

Chicken pepperoni is a popular variation of traditional pepperoni typically made from pork or beef. It's a tasty alternative for those who prefer poultry or are looking for a leaner option. Chicken pepperoni is made from seasoned ground chicken, typically mixed with spices such as paprika, garlic, and pepper, then formed into small, thin slices or rounds.
